How it works

Your daily examen, mapped clearly.

Each day is a focused flow: start a session, answer prompts by category, add a short note and feeling rating, then submit to update your profile summary and trend insights.

Step 01

Start a session

Open one session every 24 hours to keep the rhythm steady.

  • Timestamped and tied to your account
  • Autosaves progress while you answer
Step 02

Answer prompts

Respond to yes/no or scale prompts grouped by category.

  • AI library plus your custom questions
  • Jump to the next incomplete prompt
Step 03

Reflect and rate

Add a short reflection and optional feeling rating.

  • Notes stay attached to the session
  • Feeling feeds your weekly trend
Step 04

Submit and review

Submission updates your streak, score, and category insights.

  • Profile analytics refresh instantly
  • Weekly summaries stay in sync
